About Kiyim

KIYIM connects users with vendors through a curated fashion and discovery experience. Choose your role to continue.

Kiyim is a modern fashion platform made to spotlight unique, independent clothing stores and local brands. Our mission is to help shoppers discover authentic styles, new local designers, and original pieces that reflect real individuality.

Kiyim isn't just a catalog—it's a cultural space. We bring together a community where people can browse creative looks, get inspired, and connect with the stories behind the garments and the shops. Every store featured on Kiyim has its own personality, and every product reflects creativity, quality, and originality.

Our name, "Kiyim," means "clothing" in Turkic. For us, it means more—it's about celebrating self-expression, culture, and community through fashion.
